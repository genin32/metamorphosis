This page serves as a repository for Metamorphosis.

The mod is available to download at Nexus Mods

Metamorphosis is not compatible with Tale of Two Wastelands.

Requirements

  • Fallout Anniversary Patcher
  • Fallout Script Extender v1.3 b2
  • Command Extender
  • New Vegas Anti Crash
Changelogs
  • Version 1.7
    • Super mutant posture has been fixed. Thanks to C16!
    • Super mutant torso no longer stretches while swimming.
    • After you become a super mutant, you can leave Vault 87 through the front entrance.
    • Cannibal and Mister Sandman perks are now working as intended.
    • Super mutant can no longer equip unsupported weapons.
    • Super mutant can no longer use unsupported furniture.
    • Updated Shephard's armor.
    • Super mutant footsteps can now be turned off.
    • Fixed Flamer and Rock-It-Launcher.
    • Aviator Cap has been separated from super mutant captain's armor.
    • Ghunorn has been given default companion weapons - Nail Board and a Hunting Rifle.
  • Version 1.8
    • You can now recruit Fawkes as a companion after transformation.
    • Super Mutant Player can now use one-handed pistol weapons.
    • Major code changes; internal systems reworked and reorganized. Thanks to Command Extender!
    • New dialogue options.
    • New characters.
    • New quests.
    • Fixed Captain's armor neck issues.
    • Increased walking and running speed for Super Mutant Player.
    • Last Stand mode; random encounters and scenarios.
    • New perk - Super Slam!
    • Super Mutant Player stat reset issue has been fixed. Stats will no longer reset after game load.
    • Cobalt and Shephard appearance changed.
    • Fixed NPC bed knife bug.
    • You can revise your character before leaving Vault 87 as a super mutant.
    • Various other fixes and improvements.
  • Version 1.8.1
    • Super mutant caravans have started appearing in the Wasteland! They are part of random encounters. They will sell general junk, chems, food, and of course, super mutant armor.
    • Super Slam perk will no longer trigger on ranged weapons.
    • Reduced Super Slam perk chance from 60% to 30%.
    • Fixed a bug where you could equip unsupported weapons and armor.
    • Random encounter super mutants will no longer attack local Brahmin.
    • Vault 87 suit is now acquirable without killing Fawkes. You will find it in the locker after transformation.
    • Fixed crash after 'Two Weeks Later' screen.
    • Various other fixes and improvements.
